# Perspective

## Purpose

A Perspective is an individual contribution within a Conversation. It represents a user's opinion, experience, analysis, or recommendation, supported by optional evidence and enriched by AI. Perspectives are the fundamental units of knowledge that collectively shape understanding, reveal disagreements, and drive the evolution of conversations.

## Goals

- Capture diverse viewpoints in a structured format.
- Encourage evidence-based contributions.
- Enable constructive collaboration through support, challenge, and extension.
- Preserve context and reasoning behind ideas.

## Key Components

- Perspective content
- Author information
- Evidence attachments
- AI quality review
- Support, Challenge, Extend actions
- Comments and discussion
- Revision history

## User Actions

- Create a perspective
- Edit or update a perspective
- Attach evidence
- Support, challenge, or extend another perspective
- Bookmark or share a perspective
- Report inappropriate content

## AI Capabilities

- Suggest clearer writing
- Detect missing evidence
- Recommend related sources
- Identify duplicate ideas
- Classify themes and topics
- Highlight potential knowledge gaps

## Success Metrics

- Number of high-quality perspectives
- Evidence attachment rate
- Constructive interaction rate
- Perspective reuse in AI summaries
- Community validation rate
