# Conversation

## Purpose

A Conversation is the central knowledge object in Neuraverse. It begins with a question, topic, or problem and evolves over time through perspectives, evidence, debates, AI summaries, and community participation. Unlike traditional discussion threads, a Conversation is designed to become a long-lived knowledge asset.

## Goals

- Transform discussions into structured knowledge.
- Encourage evidence-based collaboration.
- Preserve context and decision history.
- Help communities build consensus while capturing differing viewpoints.

## Key Components

- Conversation header
- AI Summary
- Perspective timeline
- Evidence attachments
- Debate sections
- Related conversations
- Knowledge evolution history

## User Actions

- Create a conversation
- Follow or bookmark a conversation
- Share a perspective
- Attach evidence
- Support, challenge, or extend perspectives
- Join debates
- Ask AI for research assistance

## AI Capabilities

- Generate conversation summaries
- Detect emerging debates
- Identify consensus and disagreements
- Recommend related knowledge
- Highlight unanswered questions and knowledge gaps

## Success Metrics

- Active participants per conversation
- Perspective quality
- Evidence attachment rate
- Debate resolution rate
- Conversation revisit rate
- Knowledge reuse
