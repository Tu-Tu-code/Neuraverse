# Discussions

## Purpose

Discussions are the primary discovery surface of Neuraverse. They help users discover active conversations, follow evolving topics, and contribute perspectives that become part of the Living Knowledge Network.

## Objectives

- Help users discover valuable conversations.
- Encourage participation through meaningful discussions.
- Surface AI-generated summaries and knowledge evolution.
- Connect related conversations across domains.

## Key Components

- Discussion feed
- Search
- Filters
- Topic categories
- AI Summary preview
- Live activity indicators
- Consensus status
- Trending and emerging discussions

## User Actions

- Browse discussions
- Search discussions
- Open a conversation
- Follow a discussion
- Bookmark discussions
- Share a perspective
- Create a new conversation

## AI Capabilities

- Generate discussion summaries
- Recommend related discussions
- Detect emerging debates
- Highlight unanswered questions
- Surface knowledge gaps

## Success Metrics

- Discussion engagement
- Conversation quality
- Repeat participation
- Knowledge reuse
- Time to discover relevant discussions
