# Product Overview

## Overview

Neuraverse is an AI-powered Living Knowledge Network that transforms conversations into structured, evolving, and evidence-based collective intelligence. The platform combines human expertise with artificial intelligence to preserve knowledge, organize ideas, surface insights, and enable collaborative knowledge evolution.

## Product Goal

Enable professionals, communities, and organizations to create, validate, connect, and continuously improve knowledge through conversations, perspectives, evidence, debates, and AI assistance.

## Core Product Pillars

1. Structured Conversations
2. Perspective-based Knowledge
3. Evidence & Validation
4. AI-powered Knowledge Evolution
5. Living Knowledge Network

## Key Capabilities

- AI summaries
- Debate detection
- Evidence management
- Consensus tracking
- Knowledge graph
- Expert discovery
- Domain organization

## Product Principles

- Human-first collaboration
- Transparent reasoning
- Evidence-driven knowledge
- AI as an accelerator, not a replacement
- Continuous knowledge evolution
