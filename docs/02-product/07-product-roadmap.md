# Product Roadmap

## Purpose

This roadmap outlines the strategic evolution of Neuraverse from a minimum viable product (MVP) into a comprehensive Living Knowledge Network. Each phase builds on the previous one, gradually expanding collaboration, AI capabilities, and network intelligence.

## Phase 1 — Foundation (MVP)

**Goal:** Launch the core collaboration experience.

Key deliverables:
- User authentication and profiles
- Discover and Domains
- Conversations
- Share Perspective
- Evidence attachments
- AI Summary
- Search and notifications

## Phase 2 — Knowledge Evolution

**Goal:** Strengthen knowledge quality and engagement.

Key deliverables:
- Debate detection
- Consensus tracking
- Expert validation
- Bookmarks and following
- Knowledge insights

## Phase 3 — Living Knowledge Network

**Goal:** Connect knowledge across the platform.

Key deliverables:
- Knowledge graph
- Related conversations
- Cross-domain connections
- AI knowledge recommendations

## Phase 4 — Enterprise & Ecosystem

**Goal:** Scale Neuraverse for organizations.

Key deliverables:
- Organization workspaces
- Team knowledge hubs
- Enterprise governance
- APIs and integrations
- Advanced analytics

## Long-term Vision

Neuraverse will become the trusted platform where conversations continuously evolve into reusable, evidence-based collective intelligence, powered by both human expertise and AI.
