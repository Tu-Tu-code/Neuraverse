# User Personas

## Purpose

User personas represent the primary groups of people who use Neuraverse. Understanding their goals, motivations, pain points, and behaviors helps guide product decisions and user experience design.

## 1. Knowledge Contributor

**Description:** Professionals who share experience, answer questions, and contribute perspectives.

**Goals:**
- Share expertise
- Build reputation
- Learn from others
- Contribute to collective knowledge

## 2. Knowledge Explorer

**Description:** Users seeking trustworthy information, expert opinions, and AI summaries.

**Goals:**
- Discover reliable knowledge
- Compare perspectives
- Understand debates
- Learn efficiently

## 3. Domain Expert

**Description:** Experienced specialists who validate evidence, participate in debates, and improve knowledge quality.

**Goals:**
- Improve knowledge accuracy
- Mentor the community
- Validate evidence
- Build authority

## 4. Organization

**Description:** Teams and enterprises using Neuraverse to preserve organizational knowledge and support decision-making.

**Goals:**
- Capture institutional knowledge
- Reduce duplicated work
- Accelerate onboarding
- Improve collaboration

## Design Principles

- Every persona should gain value from both consuming and contributing knowledge.
- AI should reduce effort while keeping humans in control.
- Trust, transparency, and evidence are essential across all user journeys.
